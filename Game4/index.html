Spread the Trees

  • High Concept: Only you can help replant all the needed trees!
  • Theme: I used the preservationist theme.
  • Mandated Variety: This game uses mouse input, deterministic, with a 1 button pressing game.
  • Player Experience Goals: This game is a "build me up" kind of game. With the music and accompanying pictures the player should feel there spirits lifted in the good work they are doing.
  • Gameplay: The player clicks the recycle button until the time runs out! The time will always be 5 seconds, but if the player reaches the goal then the next round the goal doubles!
  • Prototype Goal: User input through mouse clicks.
  • Features: The incredible background and music. The player will become completely immersed in this game because of how high the graphics quality is.
  • Setting/Premise: Earth 2234 few trees are left to be seen. Too many were harvested for too many years. The people of Earth have gathered together to start a new tree planting initiative.
  • Story: A group found a button in the wilderness that plants a tree for every press. Now they train to plant as many trees as possible.
  • Target Audience: People who want to a family friendly story
  • Play Time: a couple minutes
  • Strategies: Click the button as fast as possible until you get to the goal, then rest until the next round.
