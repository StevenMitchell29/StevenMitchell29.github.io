Revenge of the Kidney

  • High Concept: A rouge doctor is going around town stealing evil men's kidneys
  • Theme: I used both themes
  • Mandated Variety: Inputs are throught the arrow keys. The evil men are randomly put around the screen.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want the players to experience what an easy game is like to play. Also that they are helping the world.
  • Gameplay: The player uses the arrow keys the move accordingly. Once the player is touching a bad man the score is changed to +1. There are 20 bad men and the player is trying to get them all
  • Prototype Goal: User input through the arrow keys
  • Features: The incredible background
  • Setting/Premise: The doctor is a man who has had enough. He has to take these kidneys from bad men so that his needing patients can get the care that they need
  • Story: Please see above.
  • Target Audience: People who want to change the world for the better, and are willing to do what it takes
  • Play Time: a couple minutes
  • Strategies: always be moving
