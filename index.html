Armageddon

  • High Concept: Destroy all the asteroids that are heading towards earth!
  • Theme: State which of the Storymatic themes you used.
  • Mandated Variety: There is random placement of the asteroids around the screen and the player must click on each one in order to win.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want them to feel like they are saving the planet!
  • Gameplay: There are very few actions that the player can make. The player simply has to click or press on each of the asteroids. Clicking successfully on a asteroid will give the player a point. Once the player has destroyed all of the asteroids, or has 15 points, a winning message will be displayed.
  • Prototype Goal: Randomness and click feedback.
  • Features: That it is extremely hard to lose at this game. Some would say its impossible.
  • Setting/Premise: The game takes place during the movie Armageddon.
  • Story: The characters are dealing with the destruction on Earth. Tensions are high and pressure is on to destroy every last asteroid!
  • Target Audience: Children who are learning how to play video games.
  • Play Time: Less than 30 seconds
  • Strategies: Move quickly and try to destroy asteroids that are near each other in order to destroy them the quickest.
