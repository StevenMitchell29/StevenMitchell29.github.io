  • Updates:
    1. Players now have 4 directional motion
    2. Alien enemies now spawn and reward the killer with a bounty of one life
    3. Alien will respawn automatically every 10 seconds
    4. Alien will open fire on their enemies position
    5. Now with specially made soundtrack
  • High Concept: Two pirates battle to keep their life in the unforgiven vacuum of space.
  • Theme: Space, Pirate
  • Mandated Variety: Input is through the keyboard in order to move both ships.
  • Player Experience Goals: An exhilarting head to head competition.
  • Gameplay: Player 1 moves using 'W' and 'S' and shoots using 'C' Player 2 moves using the up arrow and down arrow and shoots using 'SPACE' The players will manuever around the map in order to get a better position to shoot their opponent.
  • Prototype Goal: 2 player battles. Projectiles
  • Features: The extreme level of competition.
  • Setting/Premise: They find themselves in the alleyway of space.
  • Story: One pirate stole from the other and is trying to get away, but was caught in a trap and now must battle his way out.
  • Target Audience: Anyone and everyone
  • Play Time: Maybe 2 minutes tops
  • Strategies: Try to corner your opponent and get the most out of each of your shots
