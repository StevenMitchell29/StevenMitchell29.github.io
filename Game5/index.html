Game Gone Wrong

  • High Concept: PLayers must battle each other in an age-old game to find the mistakes of games past.
  • Theme: Something gone wrong
  • Mandated Variety: Inputs are through clicking on the boxes.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want players to experience nastalgia with player an old game and shock with what they find.
  • Gameplay: This is a two player game which players take turns picking blocks to see who can connect 3 first.
  • Prototype Goal: User input through clicks
  • Features: A game that does the work for you, only to scare you at the end.
  • Setting/Premise: In the golden age players gathered around to play tic tac toe to decide the other players fate. It as been a long time since a game has come to past and new explorers have rediscovered this ancient game.
  • Story: In trying to uncover their past the explorers play the game and find a meme of years past the decimated the old village.
  • Target Audience: Anyone
  • Play Time: a couple minutes
  • Strategies: Click in the right spot
